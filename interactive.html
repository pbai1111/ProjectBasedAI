Most virtual fundraisers were Zoom calls with a donation link. I turned them into live, multi-camera productions — and raised millions doing it.

The innovation wasn't putting fundraisers on a gaming platform like Twitch — although I built those too, for MDA. It was the reverse: bringing gaming engagement software into professional multi-camera productions on Zoom, YouTube, and Facebook. Real-time audience interaction. Integrated donation mechanics. Leaderboards, live thermometers, gamified giving — all stitched into broadcast-quality events that felt nothing like a webinar.

The work for Disney, Alicia Keys, the Biden Victory Fund, and dozens of national nonprofits all shared the same principle: a transactional ask becomes something entirely different when you design it as a communal, emotional experience.
